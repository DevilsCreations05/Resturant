Our Story

A Passion for
Authentic Flavors

Hyderabad Biryani & Grill Co (Toast & Tap) was born from a passion for rich flavors and authentic cooking. Inspired by the royal kitchens of Hyderabad, we bring the timeless tradition of slow-cooked biryani to the heart of Newport.

We've quickly built a reputation for delivering bold spices, perfectly layered rice, and juicy, tender meats. Every biryani is crafted with care, using aromatic basmati rice, handpicked spices, and traditional dum cooking methods.

Alongside biryanis, our menu blends modern comfort food like burgers, wraps, and rice bowls -- creating a perfect fusion of tradition and contemporary taste.
